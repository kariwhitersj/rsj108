How to Delete Adobe Photoshop from Your iPad (Because You Hate it)

Assembly time: 1-60 minutes
Awesome rating: 1000% awesome

If you hate Adobe Photoshop and want to delete it, there is an easy way to do so. Our method can be used anywhere at anytime, but is most commonly used during a fit of rage while completing a challenge for JOUR108: Media Design. This wikiHow article will teach you how to delete Adobe Photoshop from your iPad.

  • Things You'll Need

  • UNR provided iPad
  • A profound hate for Adobe Photoshop
  • Opposable thumbs or an Apple Pencil

  1. Scroll to the App Library

    Using your opposable thumbs or Apple Pencil, navigate to your iPad’s home screen, scroll to the right until you reach the App Library page. This shows all of your apps, even the ones that you hate.

  2. Swipe down.

    After you swipe down on the App Library page, you’ll see a list of all apps in alphabetical order under a search bar.

    Let your hatred fuel you.

  3. Find the Adobe Photoshop app.

    Simply use the search bar up top to find “Adobe: Photoshop”

  4. Tap and hold the app icon.

    Once you have search for the app, tap and hold its icon.

  5. Tap on "Delete App".

    This button appears in the dropdown menu below the app. Adobe Photoshop is now successfully deleted from your UNR provided iPad.

  6. Celebrate.

    Now that Adobe Photoshop has been uninstalled on your UNR provided iPad, celebrate with your method of choice. If you are unsure of what method best suits you, use this “How to Celebrate” tutorial on Youtube.
